Challenges 

DPhi had grown its community and vision prior to when I joined but it still conveyed the same vision it started with across its landing pages. How might we ensure the right message is passed across the pages and that the vision can be captured appropriately

Prototype Snippet of the DPhi Assessment Platform

The Process 

We followed a design thinking approach:
Empathise - Define - Ideate - Prototype - Test

Empathise Stage - we carried out competitive analysis and quantitative researches

Define Stage - We created user personas, user stories summarized the research we did to help us understand our design goals.

Ideate Stage - we brainstormed and synthesized our ideas

Prototype Stage - We designed in low and high fidelity and ran small tests internally and externally.

Test Stage - We conducted alpha and beta testing.
