Challenges 

With the constant need for hiring into an existing team or into a new business or organization, loads of recruiters struggle with repetitively coming up with tasks that can help filter through tons of applications while highlighting just the right candidates to pursue a more detailed interview with. DPhi sought to create a platform that could help recruiters in India, USA, Philippines, Germany have a much easier hiring process than what they already had.

The Process 

We followed a design thinking approach:
Empathise - Define - Ideate - Prototype - Test

Empathise Stage - we carried out competitive analysis and quantitative researches

Define Stage - We created user personas, user stories summarized the research we did to help us understand our design goals.

Ideate Stage - we brainstormed and synthesized our ideas

Prototype Stage - We designed in low and high fidelity and ran small tests internally and externally.

Test Stage - We conducted alpha and beta testing.

Then we defined the user stories for the platform:

1. As an hiring manager, I would love to easily schedule a variety of hiring challenges and filter only the top for a 1-on-1 interview.
2. As an hiring manager, I would love to be able to retrace previous applicants, should there be a need to reconsider them in the future.
3. As a candidate, I would be satisfied if I can easily tell if my performance on a hiring test is enough to get me the job or not and also if the hiring process is done from a single platform.
