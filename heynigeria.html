The Process 

We followed a design thinking approach:
Empathise - Define - Ideate - Prototype - Test

Empathise Stage - we carried out competitive analysis and quantitative researches

Define Stage - We created user personas, user stories summarized the research we did to help us understand our design goals.

Ideate Stage - we brainstormed and synthesized our ideas

Prototype Stage - We designed in low and high fidelity and ran small tests internally and externally.

Test Stage - We conducted alpha and beta testing.

Research & Analysis 

What platforms existed that solved international shipping perfectly? That was how I began my research. So I only did competitive analysis in this project.

Key Finding

Delivering from the international market was still an untapped space and what existed already was confusing.

Putting my discoveries together, I defined what type of user would be ideal for the product and how the user will made aware of the numerous features in the product without feeling overwhelmed.

The Features Hey Nigeria wanted on the platform were:

Shipping from the US or UK to Nigeria, Delivering around Nigeria, E-commerce, Event planning and a Gamified Experience

The Ideation Stage 

I began with the Information Hierarchy, because the challenge was that, there were so many features for a single app, how best do we serve them without overwhelming the users.
